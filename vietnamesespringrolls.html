Vietnamese Springrolls

What are veitnamese springrolls?

Spring rolls in vietanmese gỏi cuốn, are made with rice paper and translate to "salad rolls". These salad rolls have since adapted to different fillings and have since become a favorite appertizer and desert globally!

Differences and Simlilarities with Chinese Springrolls

Spring rolls generally differ from their two origins being that chinese and vietnamese. The main difference between the spring roll filing and wrapping. Vietnamese springrolls in paticular use rice paper as the wrap known as Bánh Tráng, and tend to include more herbs and a vermcilli noodle filling.

Similarly the although there are difference between the two, they are both beloved springrolls. Generally they are always cooked in the same fasion and additonally always have the formula of wrapper + meat + vegies, but has evolved to so much more to this.

Traditonal Vietnamese spring roll Springroll Recipe

Here at springrolls recipes ranked we want you to experience differnt flavors and develops your own likes and dislikes. So for our vietnamese spring roll fans here is a traditional vietnamese spring roll recipe!

To Prepare the Filling

  • 2 ounces rice vermicelli
  • 8 rice wrappers (8.5 inch diameter)
  • 8 large cooked shrimp - peeled, deveined and cut in half
  • 1 ⅓ tablespoons chopped fresh Thai basil
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 leaves lettuce, chopped
  • 4 teaspoons fish sauce
  • ¼ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon garlic chili sauce
  • 3 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon finely chopped peanuts
