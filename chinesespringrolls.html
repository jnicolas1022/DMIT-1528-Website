Chinese Springrolls

What are chinese springrolls?

Spring rolls, also known as "spring pancakes" are a traditional chinese new years treat, but have grown to become popular as both an appetizer and desert!

Differences and Simlilarities with Vietnamese Springrolls

Spring rolls generally differ from their two origins being that chinese and vietnamese. The main difference between the spring roll filing and wrapping. Chinese spring rolls tend to use a wheat based wrapper compared to a vietnamese spring roll rice wraper.

Similarly the although there are difference between the two, they are both beloved springrolls. Generally they are always cooked in the same fasion and additonally always have the formula of wrapper + meat + vegies, but has evolved to so much more to this.
